About Volt Electronics. Volt Electronics is a Pakistani consumer electronics retailer with stores in Islamabad, Lahore, and Karachi, and a nationwide online store. We sell smartphones, laptops, tablets, smartwatches, audio gear, gaming consoles, and home appliances from brands including Samsung, Apple, Xiaomi, HP, Lenovo, Dell, Sony, and Haier.

Price Match Promise. If you find a lower advertised price for the same product at a registered Pakistani retailer within 7 days of your purchase, we will refund the difference. The price match applies to identical models with the same specifications and warranty terms, and excludes clearance sales and flash deals.

Installment Plans. Volt Electronics offers 0% markup installment plans on orders above Rs. 30,000 through partner banks, with tenures of 3, 6, and 12 months. A valid CNIC and a credit card from a partner bank are required. Installment eligibility is confirmed at checkout after bank verification.

Store Hours and Support. Our stores are open 11 AM to 10 PM, seven days a week. Customer support is available by phone and live chat from 9 AM to 11 PM daily. For corporate and bulk orders, our business team offers custom quotes and dedicated account management.
