Volt Electronics Warranty Policy. All electronics purchased from Volt Electronics come with a 12-month standard warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. The warranty period starts from the date on your purchase receipt. Accidental damage, water damage, and unauthorized repairs are not covered under the standard warranty. Customers can purchase VoltCare+ extended protection at checkout, which extends coverage to 24 months and includes one accidental damage repair.

To claim warranty service, bring your product and original receipt to any Volt Electronics store, or start a claim online through your account dashboard. Warranty repairs are typically completed within 7 to 10 working days. If a product cannot be repaired, we will replace it with the same model or offer store credit of equal value.

Returns and Refunds. Volt Electronics accepts returns within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, provided the product is in its original packaging with all accessories included and the original receipt is presented. Opened software, earphones, and clearance items cannot be returned for hygiene and licensing reasons. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 working days after the returned item passes inspection. Exchanges for a different model can be made within 30 days, with any price difference paid or refunded accordingly.
