Volt Electronics Shipping Policy. We deliver across Pakistan through our courier partners. Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 working days for major cities including Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Lahore, and Karachi, and 5 to 8 working days for other areas. Standard shipping is free for all orders above Rs. 5,000; orders below that amount carry a flat Rs. 250 delivery charge. Express next-day delivery is available within Islamabad and Rawalpindi for Rs. 500, for orders placed before 4 PM.

Order tracking details are sent by SMS and email as soon as your order ships, and you can also track orders from your account dashboard. If your order has not arrived within the expected window, contact support with your order number and we will investigate with the courier.

Payment Methods. Volt Electronics accepts cash on delivery, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, all major debit and credit cards, and direct bank transfer. Cash on delivery is available nationwide for orders up to Rs. 150,000. For bank transfers, orders ship after the payment is verified, which usually takes a few hours on working days. All online payments are processed through encrypted, PCI-compliant gateways, and we never store your full card details.
